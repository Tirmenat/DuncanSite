Welcome to the website of Duncan Hall, the newest male dorm at the University of Notre Dame! Built in 2008, Duncan Hall has grown to make a significant impact on Notre Dame residential life. From its hotel-like atmosphere to its new and improving signature events, Duncan is the place to be! Go Highlanders!
